Jeewon made Things That Happen Near Water for "fun"
Things That Happen Near Water is a poster series of the following three movies: Everything Else dir. by Natailia Almada, Shoplifters dir. by Koreeda Hirokazu, The Last Black Man in San Francisco dir. by Joe Talbot. Strangely enough, these movies have in common that there are certain events connected to water. The characters' mirage-like dreams and wishes fade in to the pool, river, and ocean.
