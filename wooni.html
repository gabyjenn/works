Jeewon and Aylin made Wooni for "life in space"
Wooni is a mechanical pet made for the Tesserae project at MIT. Wooni informs the state of the plants that are growing on the panel of the Tesserae space craft, but her charm shines brighter than her functionality. Wooni is waiting for her zero-G flight in July 2023.
